About

KenjiMin•

Kenjimin Japanese Training Center is a professional training institution dedicated to preparing Filipino trainees for employment opportunities in Japan. The center provides comprehensive Japanese language instruction, technical skills training, and structured job placement assistance aligned with the standards of Japanese industries. Through qualified instructors, industry collaboration, and a curriculum focused on discipline and cultural competency, Kenjimin ensures that each trainee is fully equipped for successful integration into Japan’s workforce.
